About Our Sessions

Super Swimmers Swim School offers private and semi-private swimming lessons in our backyard private pool in Aurora. We offer Lifesaving Society Preschool lessons, and Swim for Life Lifesaving Society lessons (Levels 1-6).

  • Each session consists of 8 lessons running Monday to Thursday over a two-week period
  • Rain days are made up on the Friday of either week
  • All lessons are 30 minutes and start on the :00 and :30 of each hour
  • Lessons run from 10:00am to 2:30 pm with an hour break between 12:00-1:00pm
  • A $100 deposit is required to confirm lessons (send to super-swimmers@outlook.com)
