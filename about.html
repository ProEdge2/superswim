About Me

Hello, I’m Domenica! I am the sole owner and instructor of Super Swimmers Swim School, which I established in 2018. With over 5+ years of aquatic teaching experience in various roles, and over 10 + years of self-attended aquatic lessons, this is where my lifelong passion for swimming began. Over the course of the last 6 years, I have been developing my ability to teach swimming lessons, certifying myself in CPR/Standard First Aid and Lifesaving Instruction. During Super Swimmers off season, I am busy teaching little minds in the classroom, and taking care of my own little super swimmer, my four year old son Rafael. Super Swimmers is dedicated to providing swimming lessons, confidently and safely. This is our number one priority, all while having fun! Whether you are just beginning to enter into the world of swimming, or have some experience, EVERYONE is welcomed at Super Swimmers Swim School!

What does Super Swimmers have to offer?

Super Swimmers Swim School is a locally operated backyard swim school offering private and semi-private lessons in Aurora, Ontario. As a guarantee to you, Super Swimmers is dedicated to following the Lifesaving Society Swim for Life guidelines in order to provide your child with the opportunity to progress through their swimming journey in a safe and fun manner. Designed through structured classes, Super Swimmers hopes to offer positive learning experiences where your child can truly get the most of their time in the water with a highly qualified instructor!
